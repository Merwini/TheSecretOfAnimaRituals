Treeskip ritual: relocates the anima tree to a designated location. On failure, relocates it to a random location within some given radius of the target.

Psychic Severing ritual: gives all your pawns a psychically-deafening hediff (intended for outlasting psychic drones). On failure, gives that hediff but for a longer than you'd want, like several extra days.

Beckoning ritual: summons a trade caravan (maybe let you select faction or caravan type?) after a delay of a couple days. On failure, summons a raid instead.

Eruption ritual: raises an ore vein out of the ground. On failure, raises an infestation with it.

Fever ritual: gives a hediff that grants 300% immunity gain speed for 2-ish days, but requires tending every 6 hours or it kills the pawn. On failure, gives the same hediff but for longer than you'd want.

Renewal ritual: regrows a missing body part (prioritizing larger ones like limbs). On failure, also gives carcinoma

Pass into the Anima - Target pawn is removed as a colonist, stored in the anima tree (actually a GameComp). Can be summoned later with powerful buffs, but only for a limited duration
Call from Anima - Summon a pawn stored in the anima tree for a limited duration with powerful buffs.
